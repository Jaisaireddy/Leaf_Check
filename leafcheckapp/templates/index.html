Your plants need you

Keeping your plants healthy can be a lot of work, but it's worth it. Hereare some tips to help you keep your plants in top shape:

  1. Water your plants regularly, but don't overwater them.
  2. Make sure your plants get enough sunlight.
  3. Fertilize your plants as needed.
  4. Remove any dead or diseased leaves or branches.

Act before it's too late

If you notice any signs of disease or stress in your plants, it's important to take action right away. Here are some warning signs to watch out for:

  1. Yellowing leaves
  2. Brown spots or streaks on leaves
  3. Drooping or wilting leaves
  4. Stunted growth
  5. Presence of pests or insects

Using our Website can help you identify plant diseases early so you can take action before it's too late.

How it Works

  1. Upload an image of a plant leaf with a suspected disease.
